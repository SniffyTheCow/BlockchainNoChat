You will be asked: “Do you want to assign the trade bonus to the red player?”

You can vote YES or NO.

- If the majority (2 or more participants) vote “YES,” then the red player earns the entire trade bonus.

- Otherwise, half of the trade bonus is destroyed, and the rest is randomly assigned (equal probability) to one of the participants who voted “NO.”
