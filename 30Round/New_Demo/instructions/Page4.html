After everyone makes a trade choice, you will see your color, as well as the colors and trade choices of everyone else in your group, without seeing their identifiers. An example is below:

                                                                  FIGURE:

OTHER PERSON                        OTHER PERSON                            YOU 

COLOR, CHOICE                         COLOR, CHOICE                          COLOR, CHOICE

 

You will also see the total trade bonus created. Note that no trade bonus is created if the red player completes no trades. A group-wide voting process will then determine if the red player gets to keep the trade bonus he or she created.
